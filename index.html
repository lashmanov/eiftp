EIFTP 2017

Early intervention: from theory to practice

June 13-23 | Saint-Petersburg

International scientific and practical conference and summer school. Celebrating the 25-th anniversary of the approval of St. Petersburg High Priority Social Program «Infants Habilitation».

Registration

Registration forms for participation in the conference and school (file «reg-form_En.rtf») should be sent to the Executive Secretaries - eiftp@gmail.com - before the deadline May 15, 2017.

Dear colleagues!

We are honored to invite you to participate in the International Scientific and Practical Conference and in Summer School activities «Early aid to families and children: from theory to practice». This event is aimed at discussing: the theoretical foundations of the early intervention programs, the practical successful experiences of early aid to infants and their families programs in different countries of the world and regions of Russian Federation; problems in training of young specialists (researchers and practical workers in the field of medicine, pedagogics, social work, psychology), of parents and volunteers.

Within the framework of the Conference & Summer School activities the experience of organizing early intervention in Australia, Portugal, Belarus, Azerbaijan, Kyrgyzstan, Turkey and other countries will be presented, as well as «St. Petersburg Early Intervention Model», originating 25 years ago from the city’s high priority social program «Infants Habilitation» (1992).

The city’s administration chose the specialists from the Pavlov Institute of Physiology of the Russian Academy of Sciences, professor Ludmila Chistovich and Elena Kozhevnikova, Ph.D., to head the program. The program had been developed through several years in cooperation with other fellows from Pavlov Institute, the city’s medical doctors and pedagogues, the St. Petersburg’s «A-Ya» Society and the Swedish Friendship Association.

Content of the Conference & School activities relates to the social importance and the economic validity (investing in human capital) of early intervention, and organization of interdepartmental and interdisciplinary scientific, practical and educational cooperation for implementing of the main points of Concept for the Development of Early Aid in Russia up to 2020, approved by the Government of the Russian Federation.
